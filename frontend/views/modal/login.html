DABUR HONEY FRUIT SPREAD

Scope of Work : New Brand Design, visual Brand Architecture, Packaging Design

Dabur a Market leader in branded honey segment wanted extend this equity into a new product - supalHoney Fruit Preserve. It combines the magic of honey with superfruits, so to create an experiential brand for breakfast consumption.

Opportunity: Create a whole new space whitin the moming that's polar to the idea of functional and basic. A sinfully tasty indulgent fruit spread that can be used with almost every breakfast eatable.

Our visual positioning goal was to leverage use of honey with a indulgent fruit experiance. Honey dripping on fruit, purely depicts honey + fruits with a balanced design objective.
