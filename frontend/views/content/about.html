SOURABH MISHRA

CHIEF ADVISOR - BRAND STRATEGY

Sourabh Mishra is an independent brand strategy consultant and works on select client projects with Procreate. He also advises and mentors Procreate on integrating strategic thinking in their work process.

He has over two decades’ experience leading the brand strategy planning function at advertising agencies like Saatchi & Saatchi, TBWA, Everest, Ogilvy, McCann and Contract. He has also worked as a brand strategist at BPL Telecom and Reliance Capital. His last assignment was as Group Chief Strategy Officer and whole-time Director at Bates CHI & Partners India.

He has worked across categories on brands like Titan, Kwality Wall’s, Parle, Cadbury, Limca, Sprite, TVS, Skoda, Lenovo, Tang, ITC etc. He has worked very closely on positioning and launching new brands like OLX.in, ITC Aashirvaad, Ayurvedic Concepts (Himalaya), Skoda Rapid and Fruit Shoot in India.

His views on marketing communications and branding have been published in leading industry publications. He also teaches marketing communications at business schools. His recent work on Tang at Bates won a Silver at the Asia Pacific Advertising Effectiveness Awards (Effies) 2016.
